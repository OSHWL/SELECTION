SELECTION

selected documents about Open-Source Hardware, ready for you to read

the idea behind OSHWL is to document the existing constellation of OSHW projects, so that OSHW actors can make stuff in a smarter way.

having that information gathered and structured should do some good. even better would be to cultivate quality, of content (selecting OSHW projects that kind of make sense) and form (making documents readable to enjoyable).

candidate documents for OSHW SELECTION can be found in OSHWL candidates. everyone is welcome to meddle with them or add new ones!
